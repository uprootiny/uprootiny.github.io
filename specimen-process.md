## Studio Phases

<div class="phase temporal-past">
  <h3>Phase 1: Ground</h3>
  <p>Prepare surface. Stretch linen. Consider scale relative to body.</p>
</div>

<div class="phase temporal-present">
  <h3>Phase 2: Lay-in</h3>
  <p>Establish chromatic architecture. Work wet-into-wet. Let gravity participate.</p>
</div>

<div class="phase temporal-future">
  <h3>Phase 3: Glazing</h3>
  <p>Build translucency. Thin layers. Wait. Observe. Respond.</p>
</div>

<div class="phase temporal-timeless">
  <h3>Phase 4: Resolution</h3>
  <p>When to stop? The painting decides.</p>
</div>
